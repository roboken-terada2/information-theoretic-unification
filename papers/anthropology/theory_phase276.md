## 人類学の系譜

### 早期 (1800s)

```
Edward Tylor (1832-1917):
  "Primitive Culture" (1871) - "Culture" 定義初出
↓
Lewis Henry Morgan (1818-1881):
  "Ancient Society" (1877) - kinship + evolution
↓
James Frazer "The Golden Bough" (1890-1915):
  比較宗教学
```

### Boas 革命 (1900s) ★

```
Franz Boas (1858-1942):
↓ 1899 Columbia 教授
↓ "Race, Language, and Culture" (1940)
↓ "Cultural relativism" (文化相対主義)
↓
弟子:
  Ruth Benedict "Patterns of Culture" (1934)
  Margaret Mead "Coming of Age in Samoa" (1928)
  Alfred Kroeber "The Nature of Culture" (1952)
  Zora Neale Hurston (folklorist + Black anthropology)
  Edward Sapir (linguistic, Phase 241)
↓
"American Cultural Anthropology" 創始
```

↓
Radcliffe-Brown (1881-1955):
  Structural-Functionalism
↓
E.E. Evans-Pritchard:
