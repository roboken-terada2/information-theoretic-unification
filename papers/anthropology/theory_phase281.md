# Phase 281: 社会学理論 + Durkheim + Weber + Bourdieu + Parsons ― K_socio_theory ★★

Phase 280 で K_anthro_economic + political を確立。Phase 281 では **社会学理論 (Sociological Theory)** ― Durkheim/Weber/Bourdieu/Parsons/Habermas ― を扱い、**K_socio_theory** を ITU の "社会理論 K-state" として定式化します。

## Durkheim (1858-1917) ★★

### 主要著作

```
"De la division du travail social" (1893):
  Mechanical solidarity (similarity) → Organic (interdependence)
↓
"Les règles de la méthode sociologique" (1895):
  ★ Social facts "sui generis"
↓
"Le suicide" (1897) - 史上初定量社会学:
  4 types: Egoistic, Altruistic, Anomic, Fatalistic
  Protestant > Catholic suicide rate
↓
"Les formes élémentaires de la vie religieuse" (1912):
  Religion = Society worshipping itself
  Sacred / Profane
  Collective effervescence
```

### 影響 (2024)

```
Functionalism 創始
Anomie concept (Merton 1938, 2020s "anomie pandemic")
Religion → Secular society
Suicide rate 国際比較 (PHI 2024)
```

## Weber (1864-1920) ★★★

### 主要著作

```
"Die protestantische Ethik" (1905):
  Calvinism + Predestination → Capitalism
↓
"Politik als Beruf" (1919):
  3 types of legitimate authority:
    Traditional, Charismatic, Legal-rational
↓
"Wirtschaft und Gesellschaft" (1922 posthumous):
  Ideal types
  Bureaucracy theory
↓
"Religionssoziologie" (1916-1920):
  Comparative civilizations (China, India, Judaism)
```

### 方法論 ― Verstehen

```
Erklären (explain) vs Verstehen (understand):
  Natural science = causal explanation
  Social science = meaning interpretation
↓
"Wertfreiheit" (Value-freedom):
  Researcher's values ≠ research
  (Critically reviewed)
↓
"Disenchantment" (Entzauberung):
  Modernization = rationalization
  ★ "Iron cage" prophecy
```
