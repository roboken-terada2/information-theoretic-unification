## 経済人類学

### Polanyi "The Great Transformation" (1944) ★

```
Karl Polanyi (1886-1964):
↓ "The Great Transformation" (1944)
↓ 3 統合形式:
  Reciprocity (互酬性) - kinship-based
  Redistribution (再分配) - chiefdom + state
  Exchange (交換) - market
↓
Self-regulating market は 19c 発明:
  ★ 経済が社会に "embedded" だった
  Market dis-embedding = anomaly
↓
2024 Piketty 等継承
```

### Mauss "The Gift" (1925) ★★

```
Marcel Mauss (1872-1950):
↓ "Essai sur le don" (1925)
↓ 3 義務:
  Giving (与える)
  Receiving (受け取る)
  Reciprocating (返す)
↓
"Hau" (Maori): the spirit of the gift
↓
Kula ring (Trobriand, Malinowski 1922):
  necklaces + armshells cyclic exchange
  ★ 経済 ≠ profit-maximization
```

### Formalism vs Substantivism

```
Formalism (Firth, Schneider):
  経済原理 = 普遍 (scarcity + choice)
↓
Substantivism (Polanyi, Sahlins):
  経済 = culturally embedded
  Tribal society ≠ market society
↓
1960-70s 大論争 → 現在 hybrid
```

### Sahlins "Original Affluent Society" (1972)

```
Marshall Sahlins:
↓ Hunter-gatherers = 富裕
  - 労働時間 短い (3-5時間/日)
  - 限定需要 vs 限定供給
↓
反論 (Kaplan-Hill 1985):
  Realistic data → 6-8 hours
  Children + sick care 加える
↓
2024: 部分支持 + 文脈依存
```

## 政治人類学

### 政治 system 4 タイプ (Service 1962, Fried 1967)

```
Band (バンド) - ~30 人:
  Egalitarian, no formal leader
  San (Kalahari), Hadza
↓
Tribe (部族) - 300-3,000:
  Big-man, age-grades
  Highland New Guinea
↓
Chiefdom (首長制) - 5,000-50,000:
  Hereditary chief, ranking
  Polynesia, Pre-Columbian SE
↓
State (国家) - 50,000+:
  Centralized authority, taxation
  Sumer (BC 3000), Egypt, China
```
