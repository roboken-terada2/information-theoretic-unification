# Phase 434: Mitochondrial aging (Harman 1956, mtUPR)

**Denham Harman 1956 J Gerontol**: free radical theory of aging — ROS damage accumulates。**Mitochondrial dysfunction**: declining ATP production、increased ROS leakage、mtDNA mutations。**Mitochondrial unfolded protein response (mtUPR)**: protective stress response (Cole Haynes lab 2013+)。**MitoQ (mitochondria-targeted antioxidant)**: clinical use。**Urolithin A (mitophagy inducer)**: Amazentis Mitopure supplement、2024 muscle/aging trials。**NAD+ precursors**: NMN (Sinclair lab popularization)、NR (Brenner)。**Nuclear genome ↔ mtDNA cross-talk**: heteroplasmy threshold dynamics。**ITU view**: K_mito = -log ρ_mitochondria、K_aging includes K_mito term、mtUPR = controlled ⟨K_mito⟩ excursion → repair。
