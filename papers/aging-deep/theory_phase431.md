# Phase 431: Yamanaka factors + Partial reprogramming

**Shinya Yamanaka + John Gurdon Nobel 2012**: OSKM (Oct4, Sox2, Klf4, c-Myc) → iPSC (induced pluripotent stem cell)、Takahashi-Yamanaka 2006 Cell mouse、2007 Cell human。**Full reprogramming**: terminally differentiated cell → embryonic-like state。**Partial reprogramming (Ocampo et al. 2016 Cell)**: cyclic short-term OSKM expression、reverses Horvath clock、no tumor formation、extends lifespan in progeroid mice (LAKI strain) 40%。**Altos Labs scientific approach (2022-)** focuses on partial reprogramming for human therapy。**Sinclair lab 2023 Cell**: vision-restoring partial reprogramming in mice (AAV-OSK)。**Risks**: full OSKM → teratoma、precise dosing required。**ITU view**: OSKM = applied modular flow inverse on ρ_organism subspace。
