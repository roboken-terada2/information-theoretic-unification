# Phase 430: Epigenetic clocks (Horvath 2013, GrimAge, PhenoAge, DunedinPACE)

**Steve Horvath 2013 Genome Biol 14:R115**: 353 CpG methylation sites、predicts chronological age ±3 yr、works across tissues、20K+ samples training。**Hannum et al. 2013 Mol Cell** parallel 71-CpG blood-only clock。**GrimAge (Lu 2019 Aging)**: integrates 7 blood proteins + smoking history、best mortality predictor。**PhenoAge (Levine 2018 Aging)**: 9 clinical biomarkers + age = phenotypic age。**DunedinPACE (Belsky 2022 eLife)**: pace of aging from 19 biomarkers in 1972 Dunedin cohort、deceleration measurable。**ITU view**: Horvath clock = ⟨K_aging⟩^{methylation projection}、different clocks measure different ρ_organism subspaces。
