# Phase 438: Reprogramming therapy (Sinclair NMN, Conboy parabiosis)

**David Sinclair (Harvard)**: NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) supplementation popular、TIME 100 2014、'Lifespan' book 2019。**NMN studies**: 2024 meta-analyses mixed、Liu 2024 frontiers showed NAD+ increase but few clinical outcomes。**Tonny Wyss-Coray (Stanford)**: young blood factors restore brain function (heterochronic parabiosis 2014 Nat Med)。**Irina + Michael Conboy (Berkeley)**: original parabiosis aging studies、Alkahest plasma factor research。**Saul Villeda**: GDF-11 controversial young-blood factor。**Plasma therapy trials**: Ambrosia (closed 2019 FDA warning)、Alkahest acquired by Grifols。**Yamanaka in vivo therapy (Sinclair 2023 vision)**: AAV-OSK to retina restores vision in glaucoma mice。**Risks**: Aubrey de Grey controversies、ethical/scientific scrutiny。**ITU view**: parabiosis = ρ_young ↔ ρ_old mixing、K_young < K_old → equilibration rejuvenates old organism partially。
