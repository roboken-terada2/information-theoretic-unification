# Phase 437: Centenarian studies + Blue Zones

**Blue Zones (Dan Buettner National Geographic 2005)**: 5 regions with high centenarian density — **Okinawa (Japan)、Sardinia (Italy)、Nicoya (Costa Rica)、Ikaria (Greece)、Loma Linda (Adventists, California)**。**Okinawa Centenarian Study (Suzuki, Willcox)**: hara hachi bu (eat to 80%)、purple sweet potato、social cohesion。**Sardinia**: Sardinian Blue Zone (Pes, Poulain) genetic isolates、shepherd lifestyle。**Centenarian genetics**: APOE2 protective、FOXO3 longevity-associated。**Supercentenarian (110+)**: <500 alive worldwide (2024)、Maria Branyas Spain 117 (2024 oldest)、Jeanne Calment 122 (1997 record)。**ITU view**: centenarian ρ_organism preserves low-K_aging trajectory longer、genetic + environmental factors slow modular flow。**Critique**: Newman 2019 bioRxiv challenged Blue Zone data quality (registration issues in poor regions)。
