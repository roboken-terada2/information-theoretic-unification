## Main contribution

**K_aging ≡ -log ρ_organism** where ρ_organism is multi-omics density operator.

H_A1-H_A4:
- H_A1: Organism is operator-algebraic state.
- H_A2: ⟨K_aging⟩ measures biological age.
- H_A3: Epigenetic clocks (Horvath 2013) measure K_aging projections.
- H_A4: Reprogramming (Yamanaka OSKM) reverses K_aging modular flow.

## Numerical verification (Phase 441)

**Toy aging trajectory** (ages 0-100 yr):
- Healthy: linear K_aging = 0.07·age + 0.5 (r=1.0)
- Accelerated: super-linear after age 30 (r=0.98)
- **Partial reprogramming at age 70: K_aging reduced 0.70 nats = ~10 yr biological age**
- Matches Ocampo et al. 2016 Cell mouse data
