# Phase 432: Cellular senescence + Senolytics

**Hayflick limit (1961)**: somatic cells divide ~50 times then enter senescence (replicative senescence)。**Cellular senescence**: stable cell cycle arrest + secretory phenotype (SASP)、p16^INK4a, p21^CIP1 markers。**Senolytics (Kirkland Mayo Clinic 2015)**: drugs eliminating senescent cells。**Dasatinib + Quercetin (D+Q)** combination — first senolytic cocktail。**Fisetin** (flavonoid)、ABT-263 (navitoclax)、UBX1325 (Unity Biotech, ophthalmology Phase 2 2024)。**ITU view**: senescent cells = ρ_organism low-entropy stationary states (no division, persistent SASP)、senolytics remove these → restore ⟨K_aging⟩ healthy growth。**2024 trials**: D+Q for diabetic kidney disease (Mayo Clinic)、idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (DQ-IPF)。
