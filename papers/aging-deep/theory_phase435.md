# Phase 435: Caloric restriction + mTOR + rapamycin + metformin

**Caloric Restriction (CR)**: McCay 1935 rats、extends lifespan 20-50% across species。**Calerie 2 trial 2017** (Phase 2 human, 12% CR for 2 yr) — biomarker improvements but not dramatic。**mTOR (mechanistic Target Of Rapamycin)**: nutrient sensor、inhibition extends lifespan。**Rapamycin (sirolimus)**: Easter Island soil bacteria-derived、FDA-approved 1999 immunosuppressant、Harrison 2009 Nature ITP trial showed lifespan extension in mice 9-14% even when started late life。**Metformin (TAME trial Barzilai)**: diabetes drug、aging endpoint trial $50M planned but funding incomplete 2024。**Acarbose (ITP intervention)**: glucose absorption inhibitor、life extension in mice。**ITU view**: nutrient sensing pathway perturbations modulate ρ_organism dynamics、CR/rapamycin → slower K_aging accumulation。
