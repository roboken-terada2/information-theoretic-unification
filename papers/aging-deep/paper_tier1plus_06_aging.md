## Abstract

This is the sixth Pass-1.5 paper (Tier 1+ #6) — a 16-phase operator-algebraic deep dive into biological aging. We propose **K_aging = -log ρ_organism** as the modular Hamiltonian of the organism state, providing a quantitative ITU-derived metric that unifies the 12 Hallmarks of Aging (López-Otín 2013/2023), epigenetic clocks, Yamanaka reprogramming, cellular senescence, telomere biology, caloric restriction pathways, longevity biotech, Blue Zones, and AI-aging tools.

Four core hypotheses (H_A1-H_A4):

- **H_A1**: Organism is operator-algebraic state — ρ_organism over multi-omics (genome ⊗ epigenome ⊗ transcriptome ⊗ proteome ⊗ metabolome).
- **H_A2**: ⟨K_aging⟩ measures biological age, accumulating linearly in healthy aging, super-linearly in accelerated aging.
- **H_A3**: Epigenetic clocks (Horvath 2013, GrimAge, PhenoAge, DunedinPACE) measure projections of K_aging onto specific methylation/biomarker subspaces.
- **H_A4**: Reprogramming = K_aging modular flow reversal — Yamanaka OSKM (2006/2007) and partial reprogramming (Ocampo 2016) apply inverse modular flow.
