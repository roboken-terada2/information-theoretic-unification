# Phase 468: Income inequality (Piketty r>g, Gini)

**Piketty 'Capital in the 21st Century' (2013)**: r > g (return on capital > growth rate) → wealth concentration。**US top 1% income share**: 9% (1980) → 22% (2024)。**Top 0.1%**: 2.5x of bottom 90% combined。**Gini coefficient**: US 0.41、Sweden 0.27、South Africa 0.63 (highest)。**Saez-Zucman tax research**: top wealth shares historic peak。**Piketty 'A Brief History of Equality' (2022)** more optimistic。**ITU view**: inequality = ρ_wealth concentrated in narrow high-K subspace、Lorenz curve = K_econ distribution。Policy interventions (wealth tax, UBI) = K_econ re-distribution。
