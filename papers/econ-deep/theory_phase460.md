# Phase 460: DSGE + New Keynesian operator-algebraic

**DSGE (Dynamic Stochastic General Equilibrium)**: Kydland-Prescott 1982 Econometrica (RBC, Nobel 2004)、Smets-Wouters 2007 Eurozone model。**New Keynesian**: sticky prices + monopolistic competition、Clarida-Gali-Gertler 1999、Woodford 2003。**Lucas critique 1976**: 政策評価には structural parameters 必要。**Heterogeneous Agent models (HANK)**: Kaplan-Moll-Violante 2018。**ITU view**: DSGE = mean-field approximation of ρ_market、agent heterogeneity = ρ_market off-diagonal terms、Lucas critique = modular flow with policy variation。
