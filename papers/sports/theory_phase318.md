## 10,000-Hour Rule (Ericsson 1993) ★

```
K. Anders Ericsson:
  "The Role of Deliberate Practice" 1993
  ★ Berlin Music Academy violin study
↓
Findings:
  Elite: 10,000 hr by age 20
  Good: 7,500 hr
  Mediocre: 5,000 hr
↓
Gladwell "Outliers" 2008:
  Popularized "10K hour rule"
↓
Misinterpretation:
  ★ Average, not minimum
  Deliberate practice ≠ just practice
↓
2014 Hambrick-Macnamara meta-analysis:
  ★ Only 12% variance explained by practice
  ★ Genetic + age + early start matter more
```
