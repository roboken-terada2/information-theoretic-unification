## Abstract

We apply the Information-Theoretic Unification (ITU) framework to **transportation and logistics**, the second paper of Block D (engineering applications) after #39 Manufacturing. We introduce **K_transport** across **8 sub-states** — road, rail, air, maritime, EV, AV, logistics, urban — and verify the ITU axiom δS = δ⟨K⟩ to machine precision in **11 contexts**.
