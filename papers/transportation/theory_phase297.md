## バッテリ技術 ★★★

### Li-ion (1991-)

```
1976 Whittingham TiS2 lithium cathode
1979 Goodenough LiCoO2
1985 Yoshino lithium-ion 安定セル
1991 Sony 初商用 Li-ion (camcorder)
↓
2019.10 Nobel Chemistry:
  ★ Goodenough + Whittingham + Yoshino
  "for the development of lithium-ion batteries"
```

### Energy density 進化

```
1991 Li-ion:    90 Wh/kg
2010 (NMC):    180 Wh/kg
2020 (NMC811): 270 Wh/kg
2024 (advanced LFP/NMC): 300 Wh/kg cell
↓
Tesla 4680 (2020-):
  ★ 5x energy, 6x power, 16% range +
↓
Solid-state batteries (research):
  Theoretical: 500+ Wh/kg
  Toyota target: 2027 (commercial)
  QuantumScape (US), Solid Power, ProLogium
↓
2024 cost:
  $139/kWh pack (BloombergNEF 2024)
  ★ $100 = "EV-ICE parity" 接近
```

### LFP vs NMC

```
LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate):
  Lower energy density (160-180 Wh/kg)
  Lower cost ($60-80/kWh)
  Safer (less fire risk)
  ★ China dominates (BYD Blade)
↓
NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt):
  Higher energy (250-300 Wh/kg)
  Higher cost ($100-120/kWh)
  Tesla Model S/X, EU/US premium
↓
2024 trend:
  LFP gaining share (40%+ EV battery)
  Tesla Standard Range = LFP
  BYD全 LFP (Blade)
```
