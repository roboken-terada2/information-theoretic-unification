## 1. Overview

Tier 0 v3.0 is the **Pass-1 intermediate integration** of the Information-Theoretic Unification (ITU) programme. It synthesises the 16 Tier 1 papers completed in Phase 43-106 into a unified structural, dynamical, and predictive framework, and lays out the roadmap for the remaining 110 phases (Phase 111-220).

**Single ITU axiom**:

```
δS(ρ_A) = δ Tr[ K_A^(0) ρ_A ]
```

where `S(ρ_A)` is the von Neumann entropy of subsystem `A`, `ρ_A` its reduced density matrix, and `K_A^(0)` the modular Hamiltonian (the "K-state") that varies by domain.

**Pass-1 progress: 110 / 220 phases = 50.0%** — this paper marks the halfway point of the Pass-1 (interpretive) phase of the programme.

## 10. Honest Framing

Tier 0 v3.0 is a **Pass-1 interpretive synthesis** and an **organizational milestone document**.

- ✅ It **aggregates and re-organizes** the prior 16 Tier 1 outputs into a coherent polytope + meta-axiom + predictions framework.
- ✅ It defines the **vocabulary and roadmap** for the remaining 110 phases.
- ✅ The 160 predictions are **transparently aggregated** from the 10 falsifiable predictions per Tier 1 paper (16 × 10 = 160).
- ⚠️ **No new first-principles physical predictions** are introduced beyond what each Tier 1 paper already contains.
- ⚠️ The **polytope graph**, **meta-axioms β-1〜β-5**, and **Pass-2 priority list** are **organizational scaffolding** for the next 110 phases — not derived rigorously from the single ITU axiom in this paper.
- 🔜 **Pass-2 (Phase 221+)** will derive ITU-specific predictions that diverge from existing literature and propose experimental falsification protocols.

This document is **explicitly intermediate**. The companion Tier 1 papers (16 of them, all open-access on Zenodo) carry the substantive interpretive content; v3.0 is the **integration layer** plus the **forward plan**.
