## K_falsify_climate

## Climate attribution (反証科学のフロンティア)

- **World Weather Attribution (Otto-van Oldenborgh 2014-)** — 数週間内に異常気象の人為起源確率定量化
- 2024.7.22 **Europe heatwave** 1.5°C more likely 35x
- 2023.7 Mediterranean wildfires: ~50% human-caused
- **Hansen Nature 2024** — climate sensitivity may be higher (4.8°C ECS)

## Tipping points (Lenton 2008-)

- AMOC (Atlantic overturning) — **Boers 2024 Nature Comm** signals weakening
- WAIS (West Antarctic) — Naughten 2023 PNAS irreversible by 1.5-2°C
- Amazon dieback — Boulton 2022 Nature loss of resilience
- Permafrost C release ~1700 GtC
