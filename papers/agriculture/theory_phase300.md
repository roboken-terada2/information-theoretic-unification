## Green Revolution (1940s-1970s) ★★★

```
Norman Borlaug (1914-2009):
↓ Mexico CIMMYT (1944-)
↓ Semi-dwarf wheat varieties (Sonalika, Lerma Rojo)
↓ 1944-1963 Mexico self-sufficient
↓
1960s India + Pakistan crisis:
  Famine predictions (Ehrlich 1968 "Population Bomb")
↓
1965-1970 India "Green Revolution":
  Wheat yield 1.2 → 5.0 t/ha
  Pakistan, Philippines (IRRI rice IR8)
↓
1970 Borlaug Nobel Peace ★
↓
Impact:
  ★ "Saved a billion lives" (Borlaug)
  Famine averted in Asia
  But: pesticide use, water depletion, biodiversity loss
```
