# Phase 453: GLP-1 mental health effects (semaglutide 2024)

**GLP-1 agonists** (semaglutide Ozempic/Wegovy、tirzepatide Mounjaro/Zepbound) primarily diabetes/obesity。**Mental health benefits 2024 studies**: **Wang et al. 2024 Nature Medicine** retrospective 1.6M cohort、semaglutide users 65-93% lower depression risk + reduced suicidal ideation。**Mechanism hypothesized**: GLP-1R brain receptors、anti-inflammatory、reward circuit modulation、addiction reduction (alcohol, opioids)。**Lancet 2024 Diabetes Endocrinol**: tirzepatide cognitive function improvement in T2D。**ITU view**: GLP-1 modulates ρ_mental via metabolic-emotional crosstalk、acts on K_metabolic ↔ K_psych coupling。**Caveats**: causation unclear、selection bias possible、ongoing RCTs.
