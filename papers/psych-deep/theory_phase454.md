# Phase 454: Digital psychiatry (Woebot, Wysa, Headspace, Mindstrong)

**Digital mental health $7B market 2024**。**Woebot Health (Stanford spinout)**: AI CBT chatbot、FDA breakthrough 2021。**Wysa**: AI conversational, NHS-deployed UK。**Headspace + Calm**: meditation apps (each $1B+ valuation peak 2021)。**Mindstrong (closed 2023)**: smartphone passive sensing for depression。**Lyra Health, Spring Health**: workplace mental health platforms ($4B+ valuations 2024)。**FDA Pre-Cert program**: digital therapeutics oversight。**2024 trends**: **LLM (Claude, GPT-4) used unofficially by 1B+ as 'AI therapist'** — Anthropic/OpenAI policies restrict but not enforce。**Wang 2024 Nature Medicine**: GPT-4 outperforms human therapists in some empathy ratings。**ITU view**: digital psychiatry measures ρ_mental via passive sensing、AI delivers low-cost therapy interventions。**Limitations**: privacy、no FDA-approved AI psychotherapy yet。
