# Phase 448: Schizophrenia (dopamine/NMDA hypothesis)

**Schizophrenia**: ~24M global、positive (delusions, hallucinations) + negative (avolition, flat affect) + cognitive symptoms。**Dopamine hypothesis**: striatal D2 hyperactivity (Carlsson 1963)。**NMDA hypothesis**: glutamate hypofunction (Olney-Farber 1995)。**Antipsychotics**: typical (chlorpromazine 1952、Delay-Deniker)、atypical (clozapine 1989、olanzapine、aripiprazole)。**2024 new**: **Cobenfy (xanomeline-trospium, Bristol Myers Squibb FDA 2024.9.27)** — first non-D2 antipsychotic in 70 yr、muscarinic M1/M4 agonist (Karuna acquired BMS $14B)。**ITU view**: schizophrenia = ρ_mental が delusion subspace に局在、K_psych structure 異常 (low entropy in pathological direction)。
