# Phase 487: Sapolsky 2023 'Determined'

**Robert Sapolsky 'Determined: A Science of Life Without Free Will' (2023.10 Penguin)** 800 pages、systematic hard determinism。**Argument structure**: micro-determinism → macro-determinism、free will is illusion at every scale (genes, neurons, hormones, environment, history)。**Implications**: criminal justice reform (no punishment, only quarantine + rehab)、education revolution (no merit but luck)。**Critiques (Mele, Dennett 2024 reviews)**: too strong、conflates levels of explanation。**ITU intermediate**: K_freewill > 0 macroscopic、Sapolsky correct that NO 'libertarian metaphysical' free will、but emergent agency real。
