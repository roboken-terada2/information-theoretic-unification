# Phase 478: Wegner 2002 'Illusion of Conscious Will'

**Daniel Wegner (Harvard) 'The Illusion of Conscious Will' (2002 MIT Press)**: conscious will = post-hoc rationalization of automatic processes。**Theory of Apparent Mental Causation**: conscious intention seems to cause action because it precedes it、but actual cause is unconscious。**Wegner died 2013**。**Critiques**: too strong、conscious agency has causal role per Bayne 2008。**ITU view**: conscious will = readout of decision after ρ_decision collapse、'illusion' is misframed — conscious is real but lagged。
