# Phase 480: Compatibilism vs Hard Determinism (Sapolsky 2023)

**Compatibilism (Dennett)**: free will compatible with determinism、'free' means uncoerced。**Hard determinism (Sapolsky 'Determined' 2023 Penguin、800 pages)**: NO free will at any scale、micro determinism → macro。**Libertarianism (Kane, Robert Nozick)**: genuine alternative possibilities required。**Christian List 'Why Free Will is Real' (2019)**: emergent free will at agential level。**Mele 'Free' (2014)**: philosophical pushback。**ITU intermediate**: K_freewill emerges via coarse-graining、real at macroscopic scale even if microscopic deterministic。
