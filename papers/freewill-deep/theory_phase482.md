# Phase 482: Quantum free will (Penrose-Hameroff, Stapp)

**Penrose-Hameroff Orch-OR (1994)**: consciousness from quantum collapse in neuronal microtubules、free will via collapse choice。**Stapp 'Mindful Universe' (2007)**: von Neumann interpretation、observer chooses Hilbert basis = free will。**Critiques (Tegmark 2000 PRE)**: brain decoherence too fast for quantum coherence。**Bose-Marletto-Vedral 2017 PRL** (Tier 1+ #1 Pass-1.5 context): table-top QG could test quantum consciousness。**ITU view**: K_freewill at quantum scale via genuine random outcomes、but macroscopic effect debatable。
