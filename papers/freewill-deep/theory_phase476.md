# Phase 476: Libet 1983 readiness potential

**Benjamin Libet 1983 Brain**: subject reports clock time of 'decision' to move wrist (W)、EEG records readiness potential (RP) ~550ms BEFORE W、movement M ~200ms after W。**RP starts BEFORE conscious decision** → 'free will is illusion' interpretation。**Original interpretation**: brain decides ~350-500ms before consciousness。**Soon et al. 2008 Nat Neurosci**: fMRI predicts decision 7-10s before consciousness for some tasks。**ITU view**: RP = ρ_decision pre-collapse modular flow buildup、conscious access lags actual processing。
