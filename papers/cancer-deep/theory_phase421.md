# Phase 421: Liquid biopsy (Grail Galleri, Guardant Health)

**Liquid biopsy** = blood test for circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) or cells。**Grail Galleri (2021 launch)**: multi-cancer early detection (MCED)、50+ cancer types、$949 per test、NHS-Galleri UK trial 140K participants 2024 update。**Guardant Health (2024.6 FDA approved Shield)**: colorectal cancer screening、first ctDNA blood test for screening。**Natera, Foundation Medicine, GuardantOMNI**: existing players。**SCAN (Stage-specific Categorization And Numerical)**: AI + ctDNA analysis。**ITU view**: ctDNA = leak from ρ_tumor into ρ_blood、K_blood^cancer = -log ρ_blood^cancer-fraction、early detection ⇔ ⟨K_blood^cancer⟩ > threshold。**Sensitivity 2024**: stage I 16-50%、stage IV 90%+。
