# Phase 422: Cancer atlases (HCA, HuBMAP, Pan-Cancer Atlas)

**Human Cell Atlas (HCA、2016-)**: 200+ institutions、20B cells profile goal、Sarah Teichmann (Wellcome Sanger) + Aviv Regev (Broad → Genentech) co-leads、Chan-Zuckerberg Initiative funded。**HuBMAP (Human Biomolecular Atlas Program, 2018-)**: NIH-funded、tissue-scale single-cell + spatial。**Pan-Cancer Atlas (TCGA 2018 Cell 11 papers)**: cross-cancer integrated analysis。**Cancer Cell Lineage Atlas (Broad Institute)**: CCLE + DepMap、driver gene dependencies。**ITU view**: 各 atlas は ρ_tissue ensemble、K_tissue = -log ρ_tissue average across donors、cancer atlas reveals K_cancer perturbation from healthy baseline。**2024 milestones**: HCA v2 release、HuBMAP TIME atlas (Tumor Immune Microenvironment)。
