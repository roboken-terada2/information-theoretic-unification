# Phase 416: CAR-T (Kymriah, Yescarta, Carvykti) ITU framework

**CAR-T cells** = genetically engineered T-cells targeting cancer。**FDA approved**: Kymriah (Novartis 2017、CD19+ B-ALL/DLBCL、$475K)、Yescarta (Gilead Kite、DLBCL)、Tecartus (mantle cell)、**Carvykti (Janssen-Legend 2022、multiple myeloma BCMA)**。**Response**: 70-90% complete remission ALL kids、but 30-50% relapse。**ITU view**: CAR-T = K_T-cell directed at ρ_tumor、Resistance ⇔ K_cancer mutates to evade K_T-cell recognition。**Newer**: TCR-T (tumor antigen-specific)、NK cell CAR、Allogeneic CAR-T (Cellectis)。
