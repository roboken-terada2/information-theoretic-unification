# Phase 420: Cancer vaccines (mRNA BioNTech/Moderna)

**mRNA cancer vaccines** = 個別 patient tumor mutations から custom mRNA design、injection で immune system 教育。**Moderna mRNA-4157 + Keytruda (KEYNOTE-942)**: melanoma trial、44% reduction recurrence (Lancet 2024.1)。**BioNTech BNT122 + atezolizumab**: pancreatic cancer pilot (Rojas et al. 2023 Nature)、50% response rate (8/16 patients)。**Technology**: tumor sequencing → 20+ neoantigens identified → custom mRNA → ~6 week production。**ITU view**: vaccine activates K_immune subspace specific to ρ_tumor neoantigens、effective if K_immune ⊃ K_neoantigen。**FDA pathway 2026-2028 expected** for first approval。
