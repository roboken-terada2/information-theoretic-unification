# Phase 413: K_cancer = -log ρ_tumor 厳密定義

**Cell-level density operator**: ρ_tumor = Σ_i p_i |c_i⟩⟨c_i|、c_i = cell phenotype (mutation profile + gene expression)、p_i = subclone i 頻度。**K_cancer = -log ρ_tumor**、⟨K_cancer⟩ = -Σ p_i log p_i = Shannon entropy of cell population。**Heterogeneity index**: simpson 1/Σp_i² = effective subclone count、Shannon H = -Σp_i ln p_i。**Single-cell measurement**: scRNA-seq (10x Genomics, Smart-seq2) で個別細胞 expression、scDNA-seq mutation profile。
