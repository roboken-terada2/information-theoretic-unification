**Photonic computing** = electrons の代わりに photons で compute。Post-CMOS 候補。

```

## Physics advantages

```
Light:
  - No heat dissipation (vs Joule heating)
  - Multiplexing (WDM: many wavelengths simultaneously)
  - Long-distance communication free
  - Linear operations natively

Limitations:
  - Modulation speed limited (~100 GHz)
  - Optical-electrical conversion overhead
  - Limited to linear ops + simple nonlinearities
```
