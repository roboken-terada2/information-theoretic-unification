# Phase 407: CHIPS Act + geopolitics + TSMC Arizona

Semiconductor は **geopolitical 中心** 2020s+。

## US CHIPS and Science Act (2022.8)

```
$280B total, $52B for semiconductors
Subsidies + tax credits for fab construction

Major awards:
  TSMC Arizona: $6.6B (Fab 21, Phoenix, 2024.4 prod start)
  Intel Ohio: $8.5B + $11B loan (2023, Fab Mohawk 2027)
  Samsung Texas: $6.4B (Taylor, 4nm 2024+)
  Micron New York: $6.1B (DRAM)
```

## EU Chips Act (2023.7)

```
€43B (2023-2030)
Target: 20% global chip production by 2030
Investments: Intel Magdeburg ($33B), TSMC Dresden ($10B)
```
