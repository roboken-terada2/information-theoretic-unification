# Phase 529: BAO + Galaxy surveys (DESI, Euclid, Roman)

**BAO (Baryon Acoustic Oscillations)**: sound horizon imprint at z=1100 → standard ruler 150 Mpc。**SDSS Eisenstein 2005** first detection。**eBOSS** 2014-19。**DESI 2024 Y1**: BAO 6 z bins、precision 0.5-1%。**Euclid (ESA, launched 2023.7.1)**: 14000 deg² 6 years、weak lensing + galaxy clustering。**Euclid Q1 2024.5 first cosmology data**、**DR1 2026 planned**。**Roman Space Telescope (NASA, 2027 launch)**: 100x Hubble FoV、BAO + SNe Ia + microlensing。**LSST Vera Rubin 2025.10 first light**: 8.4m, 3.2 Gpix camera、10年 LSST survey、~37 B galaxies。
