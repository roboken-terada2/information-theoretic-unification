# Phase 532: Dark matter — WIMPs, axions, PBH

**WIMP searches**: LUX-ZEPLIN (LZ) 2024.8 first results 7 tonne-yr xenon: **no signal, excludes >10^-47 cm² @ 30 GeV**。XENONnT, PandaX-4T。**Axion**: ADMX (1995-) microwave cavity 0.6-1 μeV, MADMAX 1-10 GHz。**Primordial Black Holes**: subaru HSC 2018 microlensing rules 10^-11-10^-8 M⊙、LIGO mergers below 1 M⊙。**Halo discrepancies**: ΛCDM small-scale problems (missing satellites, core-cusp, too-big-to-fail) — possibly resolved by feedback。**Self-interacting DM**: Spergel-Steinhardt 2000.
