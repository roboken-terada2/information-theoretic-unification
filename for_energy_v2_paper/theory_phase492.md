# Phase 492: Battery (Li-ion Goodenough Nobel 2019, solid-state)

**Lithium-ion battery**: Whittingham 1976 TiS2、**Goodenough 1980 LiCoO2** (Nobel Chem 2019 with Akira Yoshino, Whittingham)、Yoshino 1985 commercial cell。**Sony 1991** first commercial。**Goodenough died 2023.6.25 age 100**。**Costs**: 1991 ~$7,000/kWh → 2010 $1,100 → 2024 ~$115 (BNEF)。**Energy density**: 90 → 300 Wh/kg。**Solid-state**: Toyota target 2027-28, QuantumScape (VW partnership), Samsung SDI、Wh/kg 400+目標。**Sodium-ion**: CATL Naxtra 2023 commercialized、cheaper but lower density。**ITU view**: battery K_energy_density growth = log-linear learning curve (Wright 1936)。
