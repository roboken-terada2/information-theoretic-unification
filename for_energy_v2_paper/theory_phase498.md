# Phase 498: Hydrogen economy

**Green hydrogen** (electrolysis from renewables): $2-7/kg 2024、target $1/kg by 2030。**Blue hydrogen** (natural gas + CCS)。**Grey hydrogen** (natural gas, dominant 2024)。**Production methods**: alkaline electrolysis, PEM, SOEC。**IRA 2022.8 $3/kg production tax credit**。**EU REPowerEU**: 10 Mt domestic + 10 Mt import by 2030。**Japan/Korea**: hydrogen society vision。**Applications**: steel (HYBRIT Sweden, fossil-free steel), heavy transport, fertilizer, chemistry。**Toyota Mirai** fuel cell vehicle。**ITU view**: hydrogen = K_energy storage medium (chemical), addresses intermittent renewables。
