# Phase 513: Tipping points (Lenton 2008, AMOC, WAIS)

**Lenton et al. 2008 PNAS**: 9 climate tipping elements。**Updated Armstrong McKay et al. 2022 Science**: 16 tipping points、5 may trigger at +1.5°C (Greenland, WAIS, AMOC, low-latitude coral, permafrost)。**AMOC**: Boers 2021 + 2024 *Nat Comm* early-warning signal、Ditlevsen-Ditlevsen 2023 *Nat Comm* collapse 2025-2095 (central 2057) — controversial。**WAIS (Naughten et al. 2023 PNAS)**: Amundsen Sea ice shelf melt locked in。**Amazon dieback** 20-25% deforested。**Permafrost**: 1500 GtC stored、abrupt thaw 2024 Schuur。
