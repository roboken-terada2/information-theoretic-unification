# Phase 515: Mitigation pathways (1.5°C, NDCs)

**Paris 2015 COP21**: 196 国、+1.5°C aspiration、+2°C upper limit、NDC ratchet 5年。**IPCC SR1.5 2018**: net zero CO₂ ~2050 for 1.5°C、CDR 100-1000 GtCO₂ century。**Carbon budget 2024**: ~250 GtCO₂ remaining for 1.5°C (50% prob) — ~6年 at current emissions。**Net zero pledges**: 145 国 cover 88% emissions (Climate Action Tracker)、EU 2050、US 2050 (under Biden, Trump 2024.11 reversal possible)、China 2060、India 2070、Japan 2050。**UNEP Emissions Gap 2024**: NDCs put world on +2.6-3.1°C track。
