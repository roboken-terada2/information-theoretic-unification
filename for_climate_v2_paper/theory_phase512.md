# Phase 512: Climate sensitivity ECS (Sherwood 2020, Hansen 2023)

**ECS** (Equilibrium Climate Sensitivity): warming per CO₂ doubling at equilibrium。**Charney 1979**: 1.5-4.5°C。**Sherwood et al. 2020 Rev Geophys**: combined evidence narrows to 2.6-3.9°C (66%)、2.3-4.5 (95%)。**IPCC AR6 best estimate 3.0°C (likely 2.5-4.0)**。**Hansen et al. 2023 Oxford Open Climate**: 4.8±1.2°C (higher) — argues current models underestimate aerosol masking。**ECS = d⟨K_climate⟩/d log[CO₂]** (ITU view)。
