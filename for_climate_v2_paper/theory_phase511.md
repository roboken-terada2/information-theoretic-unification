# Phase 511: Carbon cycle + ocean acidification

**Global Carbon Budget 2024 (Friedlingstein et al.)**: fossil CO₂ 37.4 GtCO₂ 2024 (+0.8%)、land-use 4.2 GtCO₂、total 41.6 GtCO₂。Ocean sink 11.0 GtCO₂、land sink 11.0 GtCO₂、atmosphere accumulates 19.6。**Ocean acidification**: pH 8.2 → 8.05 (1850-2024, -30% H+ increase)、aragonite saturation horizon shoaling。Coral reefs 4th global bleaching 2023-2024 (NOAA, 77% reefs)。
