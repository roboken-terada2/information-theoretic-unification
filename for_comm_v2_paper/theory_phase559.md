# Phase 559: Optical fiber + DWDM + space-division

**Optical fiber**: Corning 1970 low-loss、Kao Nobel 2009。**Single-mode fiber**: ~100 Tb/s C+L band, dispersion-compensated。**DWDM (Dense Wavelength-Division Multiplexing)**: 96+ channels @ 50 GHz spacing。**★ NICT Japan 2024.3 record 22.9 Pb/s single fiber**: 19-core 86 wavelengths SDM+WDM。**Space-division multiplexing (SDM)**: multi-core fiber, few-mode fiber。**Submarine cables 1.4M km (TeleGeography 2024)**: Google/Meta/Amazon/Microsoft own 50%+ trans-Pacific。**Tonga 2024 disconnect 18 months** showing fragility。
